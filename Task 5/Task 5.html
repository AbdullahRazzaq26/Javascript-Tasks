Task 5: Number Range Checker

Description: User se number input lein aur check karein ke wo kis range mein aata hai.

Instructions:

  1. Agar number 1 se 10 ke beech hai, to print karein "The number is between 1 and 10."
  2. Agar number 1 se 10 ke beech hai, to print karein "The number is between 11 and 20."
  3. Warna, print karein "The number is out of range."
